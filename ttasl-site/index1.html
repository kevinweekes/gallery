The Association

The Trinidad and Tobago Association for Sport and Law (TTASL) is a volunteer organisation based in Trinidad and Tobago comprising legal practitioners, students and sports professionals.

TTASL aims to educate, encourage and promote good governance practices in sport and understanding of law's role in sport.

The objectives of TTASL include:

  • Assist the development of the local sport industry by offering opportunities for education, and by disseminating up-to-date information regarding specific aspects of sport and law
  • Become a recognised promoter of good governance and administration within the local sport industry
  • Develop strategic alliances and partnerships with other associations, both locally and internationally, in the areas of sport, medicine, recreation and the law
  • Advocate for the ethical practice of sport and the law
